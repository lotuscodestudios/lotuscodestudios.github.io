History of AFMS

Welcome to the public information website and online school portal of Air Force Military School Jos. AFMS is effectively a military and academic institution located in Jos, Plateau State, Nigeria. AFMS was Established on 18 Aug 80 and the school has graduated over 8,000 Junior Aircraft Men as of July 2020 To adequately prepare and produce ablebodied and well disciplined young Nigerians through employment of mordern techniques in teaching and learning for enhanced academic and millitary performance in line with global best practices. The School was to also provide and environment for proper physical and mental development, i integrity of disciplined behavior, acquisition and inculcation of good leadership and citizenship traits or qualities.
